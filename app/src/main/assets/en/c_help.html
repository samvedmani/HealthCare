WARNING: This self-tracker is just to evaluate your daily progress in the fight against Covid-19. If u face any serious symptoms, please visit doctor as soon as possible.

You can change the month using the buttons or by swiping to the left or to the right.

To indicate the first day of the infected period, just tap the day in the calendar. To remove the entry, tap again.

Colors indicate the meaning of a day:

Red – Infected days.

Blue – Recovery days.

Yellow – Recovery days.

If you tap on an entry in the list, the corresponding month is displayed in the calendar.

To use the data backup, a memory card or internal memory must be present.
